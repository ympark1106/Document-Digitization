h Definition of Plants and Descriptive Terms The following definitions describe the distinctive habit and characteristics of the most common plant materials Conifer Trees Trees with needle or scalelike leaves that maintain liveleaf foliage throughout the year and that usually bear seed from a woody cone. . Deciduous Trees Trees with leaves that are shed at the end of the growing season and which remain leafless throughout dormancy .
Wetland Plants Plants that meet the definition of hydrophyte which is any macrophyte that grows in water or on a substrate that is at least periodically deficient in oxygen as a result of excessive water content .
